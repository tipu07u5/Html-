Welcome to TS Hospital

TS Hospital is the newest hospital one of the largest Bangladeshi health care provider. The hospital is a 150 beds and tertiary care facility, delivering international standard health care at an affordable price to the peoples of Bangladesh.











About us

TS Hospital started on 01-01-2017 as 20 bed Specialized Burn & Plastic Surgery Hospital at Mohammadpur, Dhaka-1207, Dhaka from 01 June, 2017 as 150 beded general hospital including 24 bed Burn & Plastic Surgery (8 High Dependency Unit beds and 16 general beds). Presently we have different specialties like Medicine, Diabetes & Endocrinology, Gynaecology & Obstetrics, Neo-natology & Paediatric, Nephrology, Urology, ENT, Gastroenterology, General and Laparoscopic surgery, Oncology,Haemato-oncology, Neuro-medicine, Orthopaedic, Spine & Arthroscopic Surgery, Dermatology, Dental Surgery & Dental Implant and Physiotherapy. This hospital also has 8 beded Intensive Care Unit (I.C.U) and 6 beded Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) and Dialysis. A number of reputed and experienced physicians, surgeons and other health professionals are providing their best support round the clock.
